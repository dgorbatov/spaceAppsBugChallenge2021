Ommadium

Dont let bugs eat your profit. A machine learning powered application that can detect which bugs are harmful for your crop and which are not. Also predicts future bug invasions.

SCAN A BUGVIEW HEATMAP

Features:

Scan

Just take a picture of the bug. Thats it! Our app will do the rest. Using Machine learning, Ommadium identifies the bug, the app will also tell you if the bug is good/medium/bad for your specific plant and suggests next steps

Track

Ommadium crowdsources data for mutual benefit. Bugs scanned/reported are added to a map where mutual users within a 2 km radius are notified about the bug. If they can have it, You could have it too!

Warn

Ommadium can do much more. The Heatmap shows current bug density for your location. We also use Machine learning to predict future bug invasions for your area. The mutual bug reports are also visible here.
